Smart-Schedule

What is Smart-Schedule?

An online, user-friendly schedule maker. Your solution to procrastination!

How to Use:

Enter in the amount of minutes you plan on working this session. Then, press 'Enter' to confirm your number of minutes. Repeat this process for the first task prompt, and however many more you plan on adding. Finally, click 'Make Schedule' to generate a personalized schedule for all of your tasks. Then you'll be on your way to greater productivity!
